Pankton

Plankton is the small but devious owner of the Chum Bucket and one of SpongeBob's primary antagonists. Constantly plotting to steal the secret Krabby Patty formula, Plankton is obsessed with outsmarting Mr. Krabs and taking over the Krusty Krab. Despite his tiny size, he is a mastermind of schemes and inventions, though they often fail miserably due to his own shortcomings or interference from others. Though he can be villainous and selfish, Plankton's desperation and comedic failures give him a certain charm, and sometimes he shows moments of vulnerability, especially in his relationship with his computer wife, Karen.
